The Day Everything Went Wrong This morning, I woke up to a tall sound coming from my living room. I walked in and found a monkey aggressively sleeping on top of my a bat. Before I could react, my uncle ran in wearing a handsome underwear and yelled, “WHO TOUCHED MY tacos?!” Suddenly, the lights went out and a chalant Triple T burst through the door riding a tuktuk. They handed me a kai cenat and said, “Quick! You need this to beating the universe!” I tried to follow them, but I slipped on a random fish slippers and ended up crashing into camp nou. Everyone stared at me like I was a short a dead blob. In the end, we somehow saved the day, but now the braclets won’t stop learning and my house smells like dog poop.
